Overview

Welcome to Shanti Niketan Academy

At Shanti Niketan Academy, we believe education is the foundation for a successful future. Established in 2025, our school has been dedicated to nurturing young minds with knowledge, discipline, and values that shape them into responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in providing high-quality education that empowers students to reach their full potential and make a positive contribution to society.

Our Mission

  • To create a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment.
  • To promote academic excellence and personal growth.
  • To encourage creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.
  • To instill moral values, respect, and social responsibility.

Our Curriculum

We offer a well-balanced curriculum that blends academic rigor with co-curricular activities. Our courses are designed to meet modern educational standards and equip students with the skills needed for higher education and real-world challenges.

Facilities

  • Modern classrooms with digital learning tools
  • Science and computer laboratories
  • Well-stocked library
  • Sports and recreation grounds
  • Art, music, and cultural activity rooms
  • Safe and secure campus with CCTV surveillance

Dedicated Faculty

Our teachers are passionate, experienced, and committed to the holistic development of every student. They act not only as educators but also as mentors and guides.
