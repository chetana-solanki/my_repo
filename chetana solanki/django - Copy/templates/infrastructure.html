Infrastructure

Auditorium


The school auditorium is a modern, spacious hall designed for cultural events, seminars, and annual functions. Equipped with advanced sound and lighting systems, it offers a comfortable atmosphere for performances and gatherings. It serves as a vibrant hub where students showcase talents, creativity, and learning through various stage activities

Swimming Pool


The school swimming pool provides students with a safe and hygienic environment to learn and practice swimming. Supervised by trained instructors, it encourages physical fitness, discipline, and confidence in water. Modern filtration systems ensure cleanliness, while regular training sessions help students develop essential swimming skills and healthy recreational habits.

Computer Lab


The school computer lab is equipped with modern systems and high-speed internet to support digital learning. Students gain practical knowledge of computer applications, programming, and technology. With updated software and guided supervision, the lab creates an interactive learning environment that enhances skills and prepares students for future challenges.

Science Lab


The school science lab is well-equipped with modern instruments and safety measures to conduct experiments in physics, chemistry, and biology. It provides hands-on learning experiences that strengthen scientific concepts. Under proper guidance, students develop curiosity, critical thinking, and practical skills, making science both engaging and meaningful in daily life.

Library


The school library is a rich resource center with a wide collection of books, journals, and digital materials. It provides a quiet and inspiring environment for reading and research. Students cultivate knowledge, creativity, and a love for learning while developing strong reading habits and independent study skills.

Bus Facilities


The school provides safe and comfortable bus services for students across various routes. Each bus is equipped with GPS tracking and follows strict safety standards. Trained drivers and attendants ensure punctuality and discipline, making daily travel convenient and secure for students while giving parents peace of mind.
